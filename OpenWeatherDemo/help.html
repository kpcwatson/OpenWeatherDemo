OpenWeatherDemo Help

This page will guide you on how to use OpenWeatherDemo

Add Locations

To get started, you need to add a location. Do so with the following steps:

  1. Tap the "Add Location" button on the home screen to bring up a map.
  2. When prompted to allow location access to the app, you should select "Allow" so you can see your current location in the map.
  3. The map is interactive and you can zoom in/out or pan the map area.
  4. Select your location of interest on the map with a single touch to drop a pin. You may adjust the pin as desired.
  5. Once you are finished selecting your location, tap the "Back" button or use the swipe navigation gesture to go back to the home screen.
  6. That's it! You now have a location saved to your home screen.

View Detailed Conditions

The home screen only shows a brief summary of the current conditions for each of your saved locations. You may tap on a location to see a more detailed description of the current forecast.
